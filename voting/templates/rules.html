Election Rules

Please read and follow these rules carefully to ensure a fair election process.

General Guidelines

  1. All voters must be registered students.
  2. Each voter can vote only once per position.
  3. Campaigning must follow college guidelines.
  4. No bribery or coercion is allowed.
  5. Results will be final once certified.

Voter Responsibilities

  • Keep your login credentials secure.
  • Verify your vote before submission.
  • Report any suspicious activity immediately.

Candidate Responsibilities

  • Maintain campaign decorum.
  • Submit all required documents on time.
  • Adhere to spending limits if applicable.
Violation of these rules may result in disqualification or disciplinary action.
